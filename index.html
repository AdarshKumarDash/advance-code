About


DPS RAIPUR

DPS Raipur provides good quality, modern education, including a strong component of culture, inculcation of values, awareness of environment, adventure activities and physical education. New programs and innovative practices and activities are organized as a part of curriculum transaction for setting and maintaining higher standards of students’ performance.


DPS Raipur, situated on the outskirts of Raipur city, creates a congenial and conducive atmosphere for learning and promoting the budding talents. It has made impressive strides since its inception. It has launched a vigorous drive to provide quality education coupled with holistic development. Led by a powerful vision, it is going to leverage its strengths and overcome its shortcomings.


DPS Raipur fraternity has synchronized with parent community to foster and nurture child development which will prove to be the foundation stone for benchmark performance in the coming years. DPS Raipur is committed to setting high standards in its pursuit of excellence and remaining sensitive to the issues of human resources, for their nurturing, grooming, enrichment and development.


We firmly believe that “giving back is a means towards going ahead”. DPS Raipur has made a maiden foray into the capital city of Chhattisgarh. We believe this will be the beginning of a long and mutually beneficial relationship. It accepts the recognition with responsibility and charts a future course – one that sets higher targets and inspires greater success. Our endeavor is to see that DPS can… AND ...DPS will
